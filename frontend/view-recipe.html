Roast Beef Horseradish Roll-Ups

A delicious appetizer perfect for any occasion.

Prep Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 15 mins | Servings: 4

Rating

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

4.0 out of 5 stars

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of roast beef
  • 1/2 cup horseradish sauce
  • 4 tortillas
  • 1 cup arugula
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Spread horseradish sauce on each tortilla.
  2. Layer roast beef, arugula, and shredded cheese on top.
  3. Roll up tortillas tightly and cut into 1-inch pieces.
  4. Secure with toothpicks if necessary and serve.
